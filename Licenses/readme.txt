1. INTRODUCTION
---------------------------------------


IMSR-TOOL is a desktop application to help extract information from Incident Management Situation Reports (IMSRs)
IMSRs are PDFs, available for download at the historical IMSR data archive: www.predictiveservices.nifc.gov/intelligence/archive.htm  
IMSR-TOOL does not support extracting data of years prior to 2007.
For more information, please contact IMSR-TOOL DEVELOPER.
